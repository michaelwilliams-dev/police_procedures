Following this procedure ensures that Wiltshire Police complies with the National Contact Management Strategy 2019, the National Call Handling Standards (NCHS) (ACPO 2012), the National Standard for Incident Recording (NSIR), National Crime Recording Standards (NCRS), HMICFRS PEEL assessments - characteristics of good, APP Risk principles and APP.

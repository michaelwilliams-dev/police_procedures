DATA PROTECTION Any information relating to an identified or identifiable living individual recorded as a consequence of this policy will be processed in accordance with the Data Protection Act 2018, General Data Protection Regulations and the Force Data Protection Policy. FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT 2000 There are no issues within this policy which would mean that it could not be open to public scrutiny. MONITORING AND REVIEW The effectiveness of this policy will be monitored by measuring compliancy levels from the Force Performance team for officers and staff completing their annual Personal Safety Training. This policy will also be monitored against changes in the Personal Safety Guidance document and the ACPO Personal Safety Manual. This policy will be reviewed on an annual basis prior to the completion of the design for the following years training by the Training Delivery Manager and the OSPO Supervisor. There will be on going monitoring in relation to the following indicators:  Assaults and injuries to officers and staff (Form 230)  Complaints against police officers and staff involving use of force.  Monitoring of use of form 770, Use of Force Forms which must be submitted in accordance with Force Policy.

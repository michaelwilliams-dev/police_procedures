A CSO or CSV who proposes to exercise a power to search a person under sub- paragraph (1) or (2) must inform him or her that failing without reasonable excuse to consent to being searched is an offence. If on searching the person the CSO or CSV discovers what he or she is searching for, the CSO or CSV may seize it and dispose of it.

Training will include:  Understanding of what to do in the event of a fire  Understand the different types of fire  Understand how to use a fire extinguisher safely and effectively  Practical hands-on experience of tacking a fire with a fire extinguisher  Understand fire extinguishers methods of operation, capabilities and limitations 5.3 Information to Contractors and Visitors All visitors and contractors to Wiltshire Police premises will be informed of the local fire safety arrangements and emergency plan for the building prior to commencing work. This will be available in the station logbook and displayed throughout the building via the fire action notices.

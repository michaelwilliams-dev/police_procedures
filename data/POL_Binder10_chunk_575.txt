PROCEDURE OVERVIEW The objective of this procedural guide is to clearly define the Wiltshire & Swindon Office of the Police and Crime CommissionerÕs (OPCCÕs) commitment, procedures and arrangements for the management of health and safety in the workplace. GLOSSARY OF TERMS Term Meaning DSE Display Screen Equipment RIDDOR Reporting of Injuries Diseases and Dangerous Occurrences Regulations PROCEDURE. 1. Legal Requirements The Health and Safety at Work Act 1974 Section 2(3) requires employers with more than four employees to prepare and revise on a regular basis a written statement of safety policy with the necessary organisation and arrangements to carry it out. The Health and Safety at Work Act 1974 and Police (Health and Safety at Work) Act 1997 require the Wiltshire & Swindon Office of Police and Crime Commissioner to ensure, as far as is reasonably practicable, the health, safety and welfare of all their employees and others affected by our undertakings. 2.
